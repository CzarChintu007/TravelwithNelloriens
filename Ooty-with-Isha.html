A trip to Coorg and Chikmagalur blends natural beauty, adventure, and cultural experiences. In Coorg, “Scotland of India,” visit Abbey Falls, Raja’s Seat, Dubare Elephant Camp, and Madikeri Fort. Explore coffee plantations and enjoy the Nilgiri Mountain Railway. In Chikmagalur, trek to Mullayanagiri, explore Baba Budangiri Hills and Hebbe Falls, relax at Hirekolale Lake, and tour coffee estates. This journey promises scenic beauty and memorable experiences.

Day 1: Mysore to Ooty

Upon arriving in Mysore, Meet our representative and board the pre-hired private vehicle and later proceed to visit,

> Bandipur Tiger Reserve

> Needle Rock Viewpoint

> Shooting Spot

> Pine Forest

Stay at Ooty

Wake up in the morning, freshen up, have breakfast, check out of the hotel, and then explore.

> Toy Train

> Pykara Waterfalls

> Mysore Palace

Start the return journey to the railway station after visiting the abovementioned places.
