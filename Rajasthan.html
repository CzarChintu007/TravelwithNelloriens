Rajasthan, the “Land of Kings,” is rich in history, culture, and colour. Visit for its stunning palaces, majestic forts, vibrant festivals, and desert landscapes. Experience cities like Jaipur, Udaipur, and Jodhpur, where every street whispers stories of the past. A trip here is like stepping into a royal fairy tale.

Day 1: Jaipur Arrival

Arrival to Jaipur and meet our representative, Board the pre-hired vehicle and proceed to the hotel for check-in (However standard check-in time 12PM). After freshen up proceed for full day Jaipur Sight seeing, Places we can cover,

> Hawa Mahal

> Jantar Mantar

> City Palace Museum

> Govindev Temple

> Jal Mahal

> Local Market for shopping

Back to Hotel and rest after a busy day.

Day 2: Jaipur - Ajmer - Pushkar

Have Breakfast, check-out from the hotel and start to Pushkar,

> Amer Fort

> Brahma Temple

> Ajmer Dargah

Reach stay at Pushkar.

Day 3: Pushkar - Jodhpur

Have breakfast, check out from the hotel and board the vehicle, start to Jodhpur (Blue City)

> some temples in Pushkar then later visit,

> Clock Tower

> Local Market

Reach to stay at Jodhpur and rest for the night.

Day 4: Jodhpur - Udaipur

Have breakfast, Check out from the hotel and board the vehicle and visit,

> Mehrangarh fort

> Jaswanth Thada Museum

> Ummaid Palace Museum

Then board the vehicle and start to Udaipur. Stay at Udaipur.

Day 5: Udaipur Sightseeing

Have breakfast and later full day Udaipur sightseeing

> City Palace Museum

> Jagadish temple

> Sahelion ki Bari

> Bhartiya Lok Kala mandir

> Pratap memorial

> Pichhola lake

After exploring all these places back stay and rest.

Day 6: Departure

Have breakfast, check-out from the hotel, do some shopping and later drop at Udaipur Railway Station / Airport.
